Our History

JP Furniture began as a small, family-owned business in 1990, specializing in handcrafted wooden furniture. Founded by John Peterson, the company was built on a foundation of quality craftsmanship and exceptional customer service.

Over the years, JP Furniture expanded its offerings to include modern and contemporary designs, combining tradition with innovation. By the early 2000s, JP Furniture became a trusted name in the furniture industry, known for its unique designs and sustainable practices.

Today, JP Furniture operates across multiple locations, continuing to bring style and comfort to homes and offices while staying true to its roots of quality and care.

Our journey is a testament to our commitment to excellence and the trust of our valued customers.
